# Risk Assessment Framework - Wholesale Product Screening

## Overview
This framework helps you identify and avoid high-risk products that could result in account suspension, legal issues, financial losses, or customer complaints.

**Rule**: When in doubt, skip the product. There are thousands of opportunities - don't risk your account on a questionable one.

---

### üö® HIGH RISK - AVOID

## RISK CATEGORY 2: RETURN RISK

### üö® HIGH RISK - AVOID OR MINIMIZE

‚ùå **High Return Rate Categories** (>20% returns):

‚ùå **Fragile/Breakage-Prone Items**:

**Why risky**: Amazon FBA handling can be rough. High breakage = high returns + refund costs.

‚ùå **Complex Assembly Required**:
- Furniture requiring extensive assembly

**Why risky**: Customers often return due to difficulty assembling, missing parts, or confusion.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **Products with Sizing/Fit Issues**:

**How to mitigate**:

- Read reviews - if many mention "runs small" or "doesn't fit," skip it

‚ö†Ô∏è **Seasonal Products Outside Season**:
- Christmas decorations in February
- Pool supplies in November

**How to mitigate**:
- Only buy seasonal products 2-3 months before peak season
- Avoid buying large quantities out-of-season (storage fees accumulate)

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **Consumables with Clear Use Case**:
- Pet treats, grooming supplies
- Office supplies (pens, paper, folders)
- Household cleaning supplies

‚úÖ **Durable Goods, Simple Use**:
- Basic kitchen tools (spatulas, tongs, measuring cups)
- Basic hand tools

## RISK CATEGORY 3: PRICE WAR RISK

### üö® HIGH RISK - SKIP

‚ùå **Signs of Active Price War**:
- Price has dropped >20% in the last 90 days (Keepa chart shows steep decline)
- 15+ sellers on the listing
- Buy Box price changes daily
- Many sellers at or below wholesale cost (checked via CamelCamelCamel or Keepa)

**Why risky**: You'll be forced to drop price to compete, eroding margins. May even sell at a loss.

‚ùå **Wholesaler is Also Selling on Amazon**:
- Check seller list on the ASIN
- Google the wholesaler name + "Amazon seller"

**Why risky**: Wholesaler undercuts you with their own direct sales. You can't compete with their pricing.

‚ùå **Too Many FBA Sellers (10+) with Low Differentiation**:
- Generic product
- No brand loyalty
- Pure price competition

**Why risky**: Race to the bottom. Margins collapse.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **5-9 Sellers on Listing**:
- Manageable competition IF:
  - Your margins support a 10-15% price drop buffer
  - Product has strong demand (500+ units/month)
  - MAP policy exists (prevents undercutting)

**How to mitigate**:
- Confirm MAP policy with wholesaler
- Ensure your supplier is authorized (prevents gray market undercutting)
- Monitor competition weekly via Keepa

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **1-3 Sellers**:
- Low competition
- Stable pricing
- MAP policy in place (confirmed)

‚úÖ **MAP Enforced Products**:
- Brand actively enforces MAP (Minimum Advertised Price)
- Violators get their supply cut off
- Protects margins

**How to verify MAP**:
- Ask wholesaler: "Does this brand have a MAP policy?"
- Check brand's website for reseller policies
- Google: "[Brand Name] MAP policy"

## RISK CATEGORY 4: COUNTERFEIT RISK

### üö® HIGH RISK - ONLY BUY FROM MANUFACTURER/AUTHORIZED DISTRIBUTOR

‚ùå **Categories Flooded with Counterfeits**:
- Phone accessories (especially Apple Lightning cables, AirPods, chargers)
- Cosmetics (especially luxury brands)
- Designer apparel/accessories
- SD cards, USB drives (many fakes on Amazon)
- Batteries (especially brand names like Duracell, Energizer)

**Why risky**: Even if YOUR product is authentic, customers may receive a counterfeit from another seller (commingled inventory), and you get blamed.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **Products with No Brand Authentication**:
- Generic products from Alibaba/AliExpress wholesalers
- "White label" products with suspicious branding

**How to mitigate**:
1. ‚úÖ Verify wholesaler is authorized by the brand (call the brand directly)
2. ‚úÖ Request authorization letter from brand
3. ‚úÖ Use "Stickerless, Commingled Inventory" = NO (always label your own inventory to avoid commingling)

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **Direct from Manufacturer**:
- You're buying directly from the brand owner
- Clear chain of custody

‚úÖ **Authorized Distributor with Documentation**:
- Wholesaler provides authorization letter from brand
- Invoices meet Amazon requirements

## RISK CATEGORY 5: HAZMAT & RESTRICTED PRODUCTS

### üö® HIGH RISK - REQUIRES SPECIAL APPROVAL OR AVOID

‚ùå **Hazmat (Dangerous Goods)**:
- Lithium batteries (many electronics)
- Aerosol cans (spray cleaners, air fresheners, spray paint)
- Flammable liquids (alcohol-based products >24% alcohol, certain cleaning products, nail polish remover)
- Items with strong magnets

**Why risky**:
- Requires Amazon's Hazmat approval
- Higher FBA fees
- Longer receiving times at FBA
- Shipping restrictions during peak season (Q4)

**How to check**:
- In Seller Central, search the ASIN and look for "Dangerous Goods" label
- Or upload to FBA and Amazon will tell you during shipment creation

‚ùå **Ingestible Products (Food, Supplements, Topicals)**:
- Grocery & Gourmet Food: Gated category (requires approval)
- Dietary supplements: Highly regulated, requires approval
- Topical skin products (lotions, creams): Gated under Health & Personal Care

**Why risky**: Heavy regulation, liability concerns, expiration date management.

‚ùå **Recalled Products**:
- Check CPSC.gov (Consumer Product Safety Commission) for recalls

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **Meltable Products** (Chocolate, Candles, Wax Melts):
- Amazon has seasonal restrictions (won't accept during summer in hot regions)
- Higher return rates due to melting during shipping

**How to mitigate**:
- Only sell during cool months (Oct-April in most US regions)
- Ensure "Prep Required" by Amazon (cold packs, insulation)

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **Standard Products**:
- No batteries
- Not flammable
- Not ingestible
- Room temperature stable

## RISK CATEGORY 6: GATING & CATEGORY RESTRICTIONS

### üö® HIGH RISK - REQUIRES APPROVAL

‚ùå **Gated Categories** (Requires Amazon Approval):
- **Grocery & Gourmet Food**: Need invoices from approved distributors, product compliance docs
- **Health & Personal Care (Topical)**: Requires approval + invoices
- **Beauty**: Major brands are gated
- **Baby Products**: Some brands/items gated
- **Toys & Games**: Gated during Q4 (Oct-Dec)
- **Automotive & Powersports**: Certain subcategories
- **Jewelry**: Gated

**How to check**:
- Seller Central > Inventory > Add a Product > Search ASIN
- Look for "Request Approval" button

**How to get ungated**:
1. ‚úÖ Provide invoices from authorized distributors (must meet Amazon's invoice requirements)
2. ‚úÖ Show authorization letters from brands
3. ‚úÖ Submit application through Seller Central
4. ‚úÖ Wait for approval (can take 1-7 days)

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **Brand-Gated Products**:
- Not the whole category, just specific brands
- Example: Nike shoes (gated), but generic brand shoes (ungated)

**How to mitigate**:
1. Check if gated BEFORE buying inventory
2. Apply for approval with proper documentation from your wholesaler
3. Wait for approval before ordering

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **Ungated Categories & Brands**:
- Office products
- Home & Kitchen (most)
- Pet Supplies (most)
- Sports & Outdoors (most non-brand items)
- Tools & Home Improvement (most)

## RISK CATEGORY 7: ACCOUNT HEALTH RISK

### üö® HIGH RISK - THREATENS ACCOUNT SUSPENSION

‚ùå **Inauthentic Claims (Most Common Suspension Reason)**:
Triggered by:
- Counterfeit products (even if yours is real, commingled inventory risk)
- Used products sold as new
- Expired products
- Product doesn't match listing description

**How to avoid**:
1. ‚úÖ Buy ONLY from authorized sources
2. ‚úÖ Keep all invoices (forever - you may need them months later)
3. ‚úÖ Match UPC exactly to Amazon ASIN (see UPC Matching Guide)
4. ‚úÖ Never commingle inventory (always use labels)

‚ùå **High Defect Rate**:
Triggered by:
- Damaged products
- Used/refurbished sold as new
- Wrong item shipped
- Incomplete orders (missing parts)

**How to avoid**:
1. ‚úÖ Inspect products before sending to FBA
2. ‚úÖ Use proper prep (poly bags, bubble wrap, etc.)
3. ‚úÖ Verify product completeness (all parts included?)

‚ùå **Product Safety Complaints**:
Triggered by:
- Recalled items
- Products that break/malfunction causing injury
- Choking hazards (especially kids' products)

**How to avoid**:
1. ‚úÖ Check CPSC.gov for recalls
2. ‚úÖ Avoid products with many "safety" complaints in reviews

### ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK

‚ö†Ô∏è **High Return Rate**:
- Amazon may suspend your ability to sell a product if return rate >10-15%
- Triggers account review if overall return rate is high

**How to mitigate**:
- Monitor return reasons via Seller Central > Returns
- If pattern emerges (e.g., "defective"), stop ordering that SKU

### ‚úÖ LOW RISK

‚úÖ **Simple, Durable, Authentic Products from Authorized Distributors**:
- Clear provenance
- Established brands
- Low complexity
- Low return rates

## RED FLAGS - IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION:

If ANY of these are true, **SKIP THE PRODUCT**:

1. ‚ùå Wholesaler cannot provide authorization documentation for gated brand
2. ‚ùå Price war (price dropped >20% in 90 days)
3. ‚ùå Product has been recalled (check CPSC.gov)
4. ‚ùå Wholesaler is also a seller on the Amazon listing (direct competition)
5. ‚ùå Counterfeit reviews or "inauthentic" claims in reviews
6. ‚ùå You cannot verify the distribution chain authenticity
7. ‚ùå Wholesale cost is >45% of selling price (margins too thin to survive competition)
8. ‚ùå Product category/brand is hard-gated and you're not approved
