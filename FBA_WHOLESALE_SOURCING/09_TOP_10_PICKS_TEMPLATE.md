# TOP 10 PRIORITY PICKS - Template & Example

## Overview
This document will contain your top 10 wholesale product opportunities once you've completed the research phase.

Use this template to organize your findings and prioritize your first orders.

---

## RANKING CRITERIA

Products are ranked by:
1. **Profitability**: Net margin % and ROI %
2. **Demand**: Estimated monthly units sold
3. **Competition**: Number of sellers (fewer = better)
4. **Risk**: Low IP risk, low return risk, ungated
5. **Cash Flow**: Lower unit cost = faster inventory turns
