# 30-Day Wholesale FBA Launch Plan

## Overview
This plan takes you from zero to your first profitable wholesale FBA sales in 30 days.

**Assumptions**:
- You have $3,000 starting capital
- You have an active Amazon Seller account
- You have business documentation (EIN, Resale Certificate)
- You're committed to spending 10-15 hours/week on this

## WEEK 3: GATING APPROVAL & SAMPLE ORDERS (Days 15-21)

### DAY 15-16: Handle Gating (If Needed)

**If any of your top 10 products are gated:**

**Tasks**:
- [ ] Request authorization letters from wholesalers (use template in 06_SUPPLIER_OUTREACH_KIT.md)
- [ ] Prepare to submit Amazon ungating applications:
  - [ ] Gather invoices (or request proforma invoices if you haven't ordered yet)
  - [ ] Ensure invoices meet Amazon requirements:
    - Supplier name and address
    - Your business name and address
    - Product names, quantities, prices
    - Invoice number and date
- [ ] Submit ungating applications in Seller Central
- [ ] Wait for approval (typically 1-3 days)

**If all products are ungated:**
- [ ] Skip to Day 17

**Deliverable**: Ungating applications submitted (if needed)

### DAY 17-18: Place Sample Orders

- [ ] Select 5 products for sample orders (start conservatively)

  - Total order value: ~$500-1,000
- [ ] Contact wholesalers to place orders (use template in 06_SUPPLIER_OUTREACH_KIT.md)
- [ ] Provide payment:
  - Credit card (most common for first orders)

- [ ] Request:

  - [ ] Tracking numbers

**Ship To Options**:

### DAY 19-21: Wait for Delivery & Prepare FBA Shipment

**While waiting for orders to arrive:**

**Tasks**:
- [ ] Create FBA shipment plans in Seller Central:
  - [ ] Inventory > Manage FBA Inventory > Send to Amazon
  - [ ] Add products by ASIN
  - [ ] Choose "Merchant" fulfillment to convert to FBA
  - [ ] Follow prompts to create shipment plan
- [ ] Prepare labels:
  - [ ] Print FNSKU labels (one per unit)
  - [ ] Print box labels (for each shipping box)
- [ ] Gather prep supplies:
  - [ ] Poly bags (if needed)
  - [ ] Bubble wrap (if fragile)
  - [ ] Tape
  - [ ] Label printer or sticker paper

**When orders arrive:**
- [ ] Inspect products for quality
- [ ] Verify quantities
- [ ] Check for defects
- [ ] Compare to Amazon listing (confirm exact match)

**Prep for FBA:**
- [ ] Apply FNSKU labels to each unit
- [ ] Poly bag items if required (suffocation warning label if needed)
- [ ] Box items according to Amazon's requirements
- [ ] Apply box labels

**Ship to FBA:**
- [ ] Use Amazon's partnered carrier (UPS) or your own shipper
- [ ] Drop off at carrier or schedule pickup
- [ ] Mark shipment as "shipped" in Seller Central

**Deliverable**: Products prepped, labeled, and shipped to Amazon FBA

## TROUBLESHOOTING: COMMON WEEK 1-4 ISSUES

### Issue #1: Wholesalers Not Responding

**Solution**:
- Follow up with phone calls (many small wholesalers prefer phone over email)
- Try different wholesalers (you need to contact 20+ to get 5-10 responsive ones)
- Join wholesale sourcing communities (Facebook groups, Reddit) to find leads

---

### Issue #2: All Good Products Are Gated

**Solution**:
- Focus on ungated categories first (office, home/kitchen non-brand, pet supplies non-brand)
- Apply for ungating with proper documentation (authorization letters + invoices)
- Start with ungated products to build cash flow, then tackle gated categories

---

### Issue #3: Margins Aren't As Good As Expected

**Solution**:
- Double-check fee calculations (use Amazon's FBA Revenue Calculator)
- Factor in ALL costs (shipping, prep, storage, returns)
- Adjust pricing if you can (raise price slightly if you're winning Buy Box)
- If margins are truly thin, move on to different products

---

### Issue #4: Products Not Selling As Fast As Jungle Scout Predicted

**Solution**:
- Sales estimates are estimates, not guarantees
- Give it 2-3 weeks before judging
- Check if you're winning Buy Box (if not, lower price slightly)
- Verify BSR hasn't spiked (product demand may have dropped)
- If still slow after 30 days, liquidate and move on

---

### Issue #5: Got Suspended / Inauthentic Claim

**Solution**:
- **DO NOT PANIC** - this is resolvable if you have proper documentation
- Gather invoices from your wholesaler showing:
  - Supplier name, your name, product details, quantities, dates
- Provide authorization letters (if you have them)
- Write a Plan of Action (POA) to Amazon explaining:
  - Where you sourced the product (authorized distributor)
  - How you verified authenticity (UPC match, direct from supplier)
  - Steps you've taken to prevent future issues
- Submit via Seller Central > Performance > Account Health > Appeal
- Most suspensions are lifted within 1-7 days if documentation is solid
