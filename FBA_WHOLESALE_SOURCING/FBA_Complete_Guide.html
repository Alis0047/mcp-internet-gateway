‚ö†Ô∏è THIS IS THE MOST CRITICAL STEP

Get this wrong = Account suspension, returns, complaints

The 5-Step Matching Process

Step 1: Get Wholesaler Catalog with UPCs

Request Excel/CSV with: SKU, Brand, Product Name, UPC/GTIN, Price, Case Pack

Step 2: Look Up UPC on Amazon

  1. Go to Amazon.com
  2. Paste UPC into search bar
  3. Find the matching product
  4. Copy ASIN from URL (amazon.com/dp/B08EXAMPLE)

Step 3: Verify 100% Exact Match

Pack Count‚úÖ Same quantity (1-pack vs. 6-pack)

Color/Variant‚úÖ Same variant
Images‚úÖ Same product photos

Step 4: Check for Gating

  1. Go to Seller Central > Inventory > Add a Product
  2. Search for ASIN
  3. Look for:
    • ‚úÖ "Sell this product" = Ungated
    • ‚ö†Ô∏è "Request Approval" = Gated (need authorization)
    • ‚ùå "Not approved" = Hard restricted

9. RISK ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

7 Risk Categories to Screen

1. IP Risk (Intellectual Property)

HIGH RISK - AVOID:

  • Nike, Adidas, Apple, Sony, LEGO, Disney, Marvel
  • Licensed characters (Disney, Pokemon, Harry Potter)
  • Designer brands (Ray-Ban, Yeti, Supreme)

2. Return Risk

HIGH RETURN CATEGORIES (>20%):

  • Apparel & shoes (20-30% returns)
  • Electronics (15-25%)
  • Fragile items (glass, ceramics)
  • Complex assembly required

3. Price War Risk

SKIP IF:

  • 15+ sellers on listing
  • Price dropped >20% in 90 days
  • Wholesaler is also selling on Amazon

4. Counterfeit Risk

ONLY BUY FROM AUTHORIZED DISTRIBUTOR:

  • Phone accessories (especially Apple)
  • Luxury cosmetics
  • SD cards, USB drives
  • Batteries (brand name)

5. Hazmat Risk

REQUIRES APPROVAL + HIGHER FEES:

  • Aerosol cans
  • Lithium batteries
  • Flammable liquids
  • Products with strong magnets

6. Gating Risk

GATED CATEGORIES (require approval):

  • Grocery & Gourmet Food
  • Health & Personal Care (topical)
  • Beauty (major brands)
  • Baby Products (some brands)
  • Toys & Games (Q4 seasonal gating)

7. Account Health Risk

AVOID IF:

  • Product has "inauthentic" claims in reviews
  • Product has been recalled (check cpsc.gov)
  • Cannot verify distribution chain
  • Wholesaler cannot provide Amazon-compliant invoices

Risk Scoring

For each product, count YES answers:

  • ‚òê Product from authorized distributor
  • ‚òê Brand NOT known for IP enforcement
  • ‚òê Return rate <10%
  • ‚òê Product is durable (not fragile)
  • ‚òê Fewer than 10 sellers
  • ‚òê Price stable (¬±10% over 90 days)
  • ‚òê NOT hazmat
  • ‚òê Ungated OR approval available
  • ‚òê NOT recalled
  • ‚òê Wholesaler NOT selling on Amazon

Scoring:

  • 8-10 YES: ‚úÖ LOW RISK - Proceed
  • 5-7 YES: ‚ö†Ô∏è MODERATE RISK - Proceed with caution
  • <5 YES: ‚ùå HIGH RISK - Skip
